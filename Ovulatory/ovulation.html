The Ovulation Phase

Days 14-18 | Inner Summer

Hormone levels peak as well as energy and motivation. This is when you are most fertile. Exercise should be high intensity. Your skin is glowing, your energy is magnetic, you feel more social, confident and life feels better overall!

Nutrition

These foods support egg release and boost your energy
Cruciferous Vegetables: broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, kale, and Brussels sprouts (nutrient-rich and high in fiber). Zinc-Rich Foods: pumpkin seeds, oysters, chickpeas. Fruits: watermelon, grapes, oranges, pomegranate. Proteins: turkey, eggs, lentils. Omega-3 Fats: salmon, chia seeds, walnuts. Cooling Foods: cucumbers, leafy greens, smoothies.

Herbs & Teas

The best herbs and teas to support you during ovulation:
Damiana, red clover, red raspberry tea, maca root, lemon balm, alfalfa, rose petal tea, chasteberry tea and dandelion root tea.

Self-Care

Take advantage of your high energy
Socialise, have a spa day, get a haircut, do high intensity exercises, wear a face mask, go shopping and do morning walks.

Movement

High intensity workouts
Your energy and motivation peaks during the ovulation phase. Take advantage of that by doing high intensity workouts like cardio, any form of HIIT, running, circuit training, weight lifting, kick boxing, CrossFit, inclined walking, total body resistance training, sprints, jump rope, boxing, spin classes and dance.

Lifestyle & Productivity

Be bold and tackle big projects
Participate in group work, attend conferences or networking events, speak publicly, have hard conversations, (your confidence and communication skills are at their peak during ovulation, and you are more motivated). Enjoy creative activities like painting, socialise with friends, collaborate, and finalise a huge project.
