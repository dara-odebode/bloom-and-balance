Explore Cycles

Menstrual Phase

During this phase, your body sheds the uterine lining. It's a time for rest and reflection.

Follicular Phase

Your body starts to prepare for ovulation. Energy levels rise, making it a great time for new projects.

Ovulatory Phase

This is when you are most fertile. Your energy peaks, and it's a good time for socializing.

Luteal Phase

Your body prepares for menstruation again. It's a time to wind down and focus on self-care.
